FAQ

Common questions

Are these really free?
Yes, genuinely free — all 7 of them. Every tool is a $0 download on Gumroad: Solo Job Search OS, Solo Dev Invoice, Solo Dev Contract OS, Solo Dev Launch, SouthpawIQ, The Pitch Kit, and Resume AI. No hidden upsells, no trial that expires. Download the file and it's yours forever.
Do I need to create an account?
Nope. These are standalone HTML files — not cloud apps. Download, open in any browser, done. Your data saves locally on your device. No account, no sign-up, no dependencies.
Where is my data stored?
Everything stays on your device using browser local storage. Nothing is sent to any server. You can export and import your data anytime — you own it completely.
Can I use these on my phone?
The templates are designed for desktop browsers. They work on tablets in landscape mode, but are best on a laptop. SouthpawIQ is a native iOS app built for iPhone.
Will there be more products?
Absolutely — more tools are always in the pipeline. Follow @CousinIve on X to get notified the moment something new drops. Everything stays free.
